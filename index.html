About Us

RARECANOPY

Rare Canopy is the India's leading Eco Fabric structure Manufacturer. We offer design, installation, fabrication and maintenance services.

Rare Canopy is the India's leading Eco Fabric Structure Manufacturer, is dedicated to providing a complete turn-key solution for business, sports and tourism purposes. We offer design, installation, fabrication and maintenance services for all types of fabric structures, Tensile Structure, Shade Structure, Dome Structure, Gazebo, Walkway, Louvered Roofing System, etc. With more than 7 years of focused development, Rare canopy products have served All India clients including public space, schools, commercial centers, eco-resorts. Rare canopy fabric structure products have been installed in more than 70 City all India with a total volume of more than 250,000m²
